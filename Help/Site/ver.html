Word verification

The Word verification dialog appears when you enter a word that Ogle does not know:

Click the button near the top of the dialog to show the Wiktionary page for the word, if it exists. If the word is valid, click Add to Lexicon to score it as normal. Ogle will also learn the word for future games, but it will not score it in this one.

Click Cancel Entry to discard the word without scoring it.
