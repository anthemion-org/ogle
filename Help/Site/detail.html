Word Detail

If you click a word entry in the Score dialog, the Word detail dialog will appear:

Click the button near the top to show the Wiktionary page for this word.

Boxes on the left and right show who entered the word, and how much they scored:

  • A Missed entry was not entered by that player;
  • A Duplicate entry was found to match a word entered earlier by that player;
  • A Followed entry was superceded before or after being entered;
  • A Valid entry was accepted, and scored one point.
