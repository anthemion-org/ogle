Valid words

All English words with four or more letters are valid, with these exceptions:

  • No person, place, organization, or brand names are valid, except those widely genericized, like Judas or Frisbee.
  • No abbreviations or acronyms are valid, except those that are no longer recognized as such, like abend or snafu.
  • No words requiring accents or punctuation are valid, including contractions and hyphenated words.
